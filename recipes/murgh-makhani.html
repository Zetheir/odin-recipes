Murgh Makhani (Buttered Chicken) Recipe

Description

Murgh Makhani is an Indian dish originating in Delhi. It is a type of curry made from chicken with a spiced tomato and butter (makhan) sauce. Its sauce is known for its rich texture. It is similar to chicken tikka masala, which uses a tomato paste. I thought this one was delicious and tried it out with my family, and it was a hit! They love this dish. So here's the way I done it so you can impress your family too!

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • A dash of olive oil
  • 2 tsps. salt
  • 1 tsp. lemmon pepper
  • 1 tsp. smoked paprika
  • 2 tsps. yellow food coloring
  • A fistful of diced coriander
  • 2 tsps. curry
  • 1 tsp. red pepper flakes
  • Half sicilian lemon (or a whole lemon)
  • 200ml natural yogurt
  • 200g butter
  • A big red onion
  • 2 big tomatoes
  • 2 fistfuls of brazil nuts
  • 1/4 package of heavy cream
  • Tiny slices of ginger
  1. Prepare a garlic and ginger paste by adding both ingredients in a mortar with olive oil. Grind them until they get resemble a paste.
  2. Cut the chicken breasts in cubes.
  3. On a bowl with the chicken cubes, add salt, half of the garlic and ginger paste, lemon pepper, smoked paprika, yellow food coloring, coriander, curry, lemon juice and yogurt. Mix and let it sit for 15mins.
  4. On a frying pan, add 100g of butter and fry the chicken for 10mins. Not a lot of time, beacuse we will cook it again. If the pan has too much liquid, reserve it in another bowl.
  5. Take out the chicken of the pan and reserve it in another bowl. In the pan, add 50g butter.
  6. Fry the onions, the sliced tomatoes and - after lightly smashing them - the brazil nuts.
  7. In the pan, add salt, pepper flakes, coriander, curry, yellow food coloring, smoked paprika, lemon and the other half of the garlic and ginger.
  8. Add the sauce you took out from the chicken cubes. If even then the pan is too dry, add water.
  9. Half cover the pan with a lid and let it sit for 10mins in medium heat.
  10. Blend the sauce in a blender.
  11. Add more 50g of butter on the last used frying pan. Add the sauce from the blender.
  12. Add the chicken cubs in said sauce and let them cook inside the sauce.
  13. To give some flourish to the dish, put the chicken in a nice bowl and add some lines of heavy cream on top of the dish. Add a few and nice leaves of coriander and mint on top.
